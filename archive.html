Our Collection

The Tamil Information Centre Archive Centre preserves a rich collection of materials documenting Tamil history and culture. Our archives include historical documents, photographs, oral histories, and artifacts that reflect the experiences and heritage of Sri Lankan Tamils. These materials are available for researchers, students, and the public to explore.
