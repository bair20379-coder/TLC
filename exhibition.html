Tamils of Lanka Exhibition

The Tamils of Lanka exhibition showcases the rich history, culture, and resilience of the Tamil people of Sri Lanka. Featuring artifacts, photographs, and interactive displays, this exhibition offers a deep dive into Tamil heritage from ancient times to the present.

Historical Timeline

1948

Sri Lanka gains independence, marking the start of modern Tamil political movements.

1983

Tamil Information Centre is established to advocate for Tamil rights.

2009

End of the Sri Lankan Civil War, prompting increased focus on Tamil heritage preservation.
